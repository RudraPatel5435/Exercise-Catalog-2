Before Workout
This workout plan is just for setting a baseline of your strength. If you feel that the weight is easy for you or the reps are too much, you can always increase the weight or decrease the reps.
Perfect form/posture is really important while working out. Slight imbalance might result in pain and doing it completely wrong can also cause injury. To make sure you are working out with perfect form, watch videos from Muscle Specific Workout Section to know the actual form.
If you see Sr. No. like (4a 4b), it means that those exercises are part of a superset.
A superset is a combination of exercises that you have to do together without taking rest. Rest is taken after 1 set of superset.
